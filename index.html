PROBLEM STATEMENT

Many parents struggle to impose healthy limits on themselves so how can we expect they care well about their children. The average adult spends over 11 hours per day behind a screen, according to the Kaiser Family Foundation. Although many parents use TV to wind down before bed, screen time before bed can backfire. The light emitted from screens interferes with the sleep cycle in the brain and can lead to insomnia and also vision problem. in many cases working parents leave their children at home so there is a high possibilty that the children will watch tv and that too from very near to tv-screen. Hence, such a system has to be build which can eradicate this problem or at least reduce it to the minimum level. This project gives us one of the most efficient ways to keep children at safe distance from tv-screen. By using this vision protector we atleast insure that children is not watching tv from much nearer to screen

OBJECTIVES

As we always hear prevention and protection is better than cure so, the main objective of this project is to maintain a safe distance from tv-screen so as to protect our eye and prevent loss or blur of eye sight. Nowadays people are busy in their works and they forgot to take care of their children and children likes to sit near tv-screen while whatching tv and that causes serious eye problem and also causes negative effect on health and psychology of children. So This paper presents an Arduino based vision protector . The Arduino Uno controller is used to read the presense of childrenwith the help of Ultrasonic sensor. After detecting the children the buzzer makes noise till the time child is near the screen . Arduino Nano used has Atmega328p-pu old version IC. . Embedded C is used to program the controller

WORKING

After setting up the Vision Protector and making all the necessary connections, upload the code to Arduino and provide 5V power supply to the circuit. Once the system is powered ON, Arduino keeps monitoring for any object near the Ultrasonic Sensor If the Ultrasonic Sensor at lower level detects anything then we look at ultrasonic sensor at upper level if nothing is detected then Arduino will activate the buzzer and will start to make beep sound till the time the object is within the range. else if upper level ultrasonic sensor also detect anything then normal function will continue and no beep sound or any disturbance made as the height is higher and detected one can’t be child.
